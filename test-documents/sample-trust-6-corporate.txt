CORPORATE TRUST AGREEMENT

OMEGA CORPORATION EMPLOYEE BENEFIT TRUST

This Trust Agreement is entered into on the 15th day of June, 2020, by and between OMEGA CORPORATION, a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), and FIDELITY MANAGEMENT TRUST COMPANY, a Massachusetts trust company (the "Trustee").

ARTICLE 1 - ESTABLISHMENT OF TRUST
1.1 Trust Name. This trust shall be known as the "Omega Corporation Employee Benefit Trust."

1.2 Purpose. This trust is established to hold and administer employee benefit plan assets in accordance with ERISA requirements.

ARTICLE 2 - TRUSTEE APPOINTMENT  
2.1 Corporate Trustee. Fidelity Management Trust Company is hereby appointed as the sole trustee of this trust.

2.2 Trustee Address. The trustee's principal office is located at:
Fidelity Management Trust Company
245 Summer Street
Boston, Massachusetts 02210

2.3 Successor Trustees. In the event Fidelity Management Trust Company ceases to serve, the successor trustees shall be:
Primary: The Bank of New York Mellon Corporation
Secondary: State Street Corporation
Tertiary: JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association

ARTICLE 3 - TRUST PROPERTY
3.1 Initial Funding. The Company hereby transfers to the Trustee the sum of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000) to be held in trust.

3.2 Additional Contributions. The Company may make additional contributions to the trust from time to time.

ARTICLE 4 - INVESTMENT POWERS
The Trustee shall have full power and authority to invest and reinvest trust assets in:
(a) Stocks, bonds, and securities of any nature
(b) Mutual funds and exchange-traded funds  
(c) Real estate investment trusts
(d) Government securities
(e) Money market instruments
(f) Alternative investments as approved by the Company

ARTICLE 5 - ADMINISTRATION
5.1 Governing Law. This trust shall be governed by the laws of Massachusetts and applicable federal law.

5.2 ERISA Compliance. All trust operations shall comply with the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA).

5.3 Plan Administrator. Omega Corporation shall serve as the Plan Administrator for all employee benefit plans funded through this trust.

ARTICLE 6 - TERM
This trust shall continue until terminated by the Company or until all trust assets have been distributed in accordance with the applicable plan documents.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Trust Agreement as of the date first written above.
