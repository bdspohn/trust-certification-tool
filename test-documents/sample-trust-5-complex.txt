TRUST AGREEMENT

THE HARRISON J. PEMBERTON III FAMILY TRUST DATED DECEMBER 31, 1995, AS AMENDED AND RESTATED

THIS AMENDED AND RESTATED TRUST AGREEMENT is made this 31st day of December, 2022, between HARRISON JAMES PEMBERTON III, an individual (the "Grantor"), and HARRISON JAMES PEMBERTON III and U.S. BANK NATIONAL ASSOCIATION, as Co-Trustees (the "Trustees").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Grantor previously executed a trust agreement dated December 31, 1995; and

WHEREAS, Grantor desires to amend and restate said trust in its entirety;

NOW THEREFORE, Grantor declares:

ARTICLE I - TRUST IDENTIFICATION
Section 1.1 Name. This trust shall be known as "The Harrison J. Pemberton III Family Trust dated December 31, 1995, as Amended and Restated."

Section 1.2 Tax Identification. The trust tax identification number is 95-4567890.

ARTICLE II - TRUSTEES
Section 2.1 Current Trustees. The current trustees are:
(a) Harrison James Pemberton III, Individual Trustee
(b) U.S. Bank National Association, Corporate Trustee

Section 2.2 Successor Trustees. Upon the resignation, death, or incapacity of Harrison James Pemberton III, the following shall serve as successor individual trustees:
First: Harrison James Pemberton IV (son)
Second: Margaret Elizabeth Pemberton-Walker (daughter)
Third: Charles Worthington Pemberton (son)

Section 2.3 Corporate Successor. Should U.S. Bank National Association cease to serve, the successor corporate trustee shall be:
First: Northern Trust Company
Second: Wells Fargo Bank, N.A.

ARTICLE III - BENEFICIARIES
Section 3.1 Income Beneficiaries During Grantor's Lifetime.
During Grantor's lifetime, the income beneficiaries are:
- Harrison James Pemberton III (Grantor)
- Catherine Louise Pemberton (wife)

Section 3.2 Remainder Beneficiaries.
Upon Grantor's death, the remainder beneficiaries are:
- Harrison James Pemberton IV (40%)
- Margaret Elizabeth Pemberton-Walker (30%)  
- Charles Worthington Pemberton (25%)
- The Pemberton Family Foundation (5%)

ARTICLE IV - GOVERNING LAW
This trust shall be administered under the laws of the State of Delaware, regardless of where the trust property may be located.

ARTICLE V - AMENDMENT AND REVOCATION
Section 5.1 Revocable Nature. This trust is revocable and may be amended or revoked by Grantor at any time during his lifetime.
