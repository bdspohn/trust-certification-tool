IRREVOCABLE LIFE INSURANCE TRUST

AGREEMENT OF TRUST made this 18th day of September, 2021, between MARGARET ANN JOHNSON, an individual residing in Harris County, Texas (the "Grantor"), and WELLS FARGO BANK, NATIONAL ASSOCIATION, a national banking association (the "Trustee").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Grantor desires to create an irrevocable trust for the benefit of her descendants; and

WHEREAS, Grantor wishes to remove certain life insurance policies from her gross estate for federal estate tax purposes;

NOW, THEREFORE, Grantor and Trustee agree as follows:

SECTION 1. CREATION OF TRUST
1.1 Trust Name. This trust shall be known as the "Margaret Ann Johnson Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust."

1.2 Irrevocability. This trust is irrevocable and may not be amended, modified, or revoked by Grantor.

SECTION 2. TRUSTEE
2.1 Corporate Trustee. Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, shall serve as the sole trustee.

2.2 Successor Trustee. If Wells Fargo Bank ceases to serve, then First Republic Bank shall serve as successor trustee.

SECTION 3. BENEFICIARIES
3.1 Primary Beneficiaries. The primary beneficiaries are:
- Sarah Elizabeth Johnson (daughter)
- Michael David Johnson (son)
- Rebecca Claire Johnson-Williams (daughter)

3.2 Contingent Beneficiaries. The contingent beneficiaries are the lawful descendants of the primary beneficiaries, per stirpes.

SECTION 4. TRUST PROPERTY
4.1 Initial Property. The initial trust property consists of:
- $50,000 in cash
- Life insurance policy No. 12345678 issued by MetLife Insurance Company

4.2 Additional Contributions. Grantor may make additional contributions to this trust through annual gifts.

SECTION 5. POWERS OF TRUSTEE
The Trustee shall have the following powers:
(a) To pay premiums on life insurance policies owned by the trust
(b) To invest and reinvest trust funds
(c) To make distributions to beneficiaries
(d) To file tax returns (Form 1041) and pay taxes
(e) To exercise all rights as owner of life insurance policies
(f) To hire investment managers and other professionals

SECTION 6. DISTRIBUTIONS
6.1 During Grantor's Lifetime. During Grantor's lifetime, the Trustee may distribute income and principal to the beneficiaries for their health, education, maintenance, and support.

6.2 Upon Grantor's Death. Upon Grantor's death, the life insurance proceeds shall be distributed as follows:
- 40% to Sarah Elizabeth Johnson
- 40% to Michael David Johnson  
- 20% to Rebecca Claire Johnson-Williams

SECTION 7. ADMINISTRATION
7.1 Governing Law. This trust shall be governed by Texas law.

7.2 Tax ID Number. The trust tax identification number is 12-3456789.
